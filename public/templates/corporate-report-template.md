---

LEGAL SAFETY NOTES:
-------------------
- Stick to publicly available data only
- Quote government/company statements directly
- Cite all sources with working links
- Never speculate beyond evidence
- Include disclaimer: "Based on public records; see sources for verification"
- Consult a lawyer before naming individuals in criminal allegations
- Contact FIDH (fidh.org) or Amnesty (amnesty.org) for free legal review
