Checklist before submission:
- [ ] Primary source links included and archived (download date)
- [ ] Data files (CSV/XLS) attached and described
- [ ] Photos georeferenced and timestamped
- [ ] Administrative acts identified (authority, date, link)
- [ ] Local/regional contacts listed
- [ ] Any personal data removed or redacted for safety

Quick evidence checklist by indicator:

- Payments delays (Directive 2011/7/EU):
	- [ ] Export of invoices with issue/payment dates (source + download timestamp)
	- [ ] Aggregated summary (CSV) showing % payments > 60 days by year/sector
	- [ ] Any audit or Corte dei Conti report referencing payment delays

- Direct awards & fragmentation (Directives 2014/24/EU, 2014/25/EU):
	- [ ] Table of repeated awards with same CIG/contractor (CSV)
	- [ ] Copies of award notices and "determina a contrarre"
	- [ ] Evidence of deliberate fragmentation (similar object descriptions, close dates)

- EIA/SEA timeline breaches (Directives 2011/92/EU, 2001/42/EC):
	- [ ] Timeline table showing statutory deadlines vs actual decision dates
	- [ ] Project dossier and evidence that no suspension act was issued
	- [ ] Links to regional VIA/SEA portal entries

- PNRR / Digitalisation issues:
	- [ ] Procurement contract for software and scope (PNRR notice)
	- [ ] Evidence of no real use (missing logs, absent training records)


Suggested next steps (we will help):
1. Prepare a short cover letter describing the violation.
2. Attach the evidentiary package (CSV, photos, copies of acts).
3. Open a GitHub issue in the repository or email us at [contact address] with the package.
4. If immediate harm is present, notify local environmental authority and press office.
